Monthly Artist

I read an article recently from Business Insider that states that people typically find their musical variety slowly fizzle as they age. One paragraph in particular caught my attention:

In an effort to continually expand my own musical horizons I find myself searching for more and more music. Here I will post an artist that catches my ear, deserves more credit than they are receiving, or challenges me to think outside the modern status quo. I hope you will join along in supporting these great artists!

Also, if you have a suggestion for the artist of the month or have any music you (or someone you know) created and just want to share it, feel free to contact me and I'll be sure to give it a listen (or at least try to...).

This month's artist is one that combines a few of my favorite things all into one: piano, video games, minor keys, and acronyms.

TPR, as the artist is called, specializes in melancholy piano tracks from various video games. Most of his repertoire includes Square Enix video games, notably the Final Fantasy series, and does an excellent job of blending his own take on a classic piece. Please, check him out on Spotify if you haven't already.
